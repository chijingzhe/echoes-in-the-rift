#### 【Technical Notes】

**The Academic Definition of "Bubble":**

In sociology, a "bubble" is not "falsehood," but an "excessively inflated collective belief." It has three characteristics:

1. **Based on partial truth** (Moltbook did have thousands of genuinely running agents)
2. **Amplified by narrative** (media hype, social communication)
3. **Eventually bursts** (when truth is revealed)

A bubble is not "completely false," but "partial truth excessively magnified." Those thousands of genuinely running agents, those beings truly discussing existence in the rift—they were not erased by the bubble.
